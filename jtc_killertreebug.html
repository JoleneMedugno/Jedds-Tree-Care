Emerald Ash Borer

Meet the bug who kills Ash Trees

The Emerald ash borer is an exotic beetle that was first discovered in Michigan in 2002. These bugs feed on the inner part of as trees. They affect the tree's transport of water and nutrients. Emerald ash bores have arrived in the United States because they were inside the solid woud of packages on planes and ships from Asia. In August of 2017, these bugs are now found in 31 states.
